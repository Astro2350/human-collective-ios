## Common Questions

### Full Archive Access

Full Archive Access unlocks the same archive at every support level. If your purchase does not appear right away, close and reopen the app, then check that you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase.

### Saved Pieces

Saved pieces are stored on your device. If you delete the app or move to a new device, saved pieces may not carry over.

### Collective Submissions

Collective submissions are reviewed before publication. Not every submission will be selected. To report published artwork, use the options menu on its card in the Collective tab. For attribution or removal concerns, email the support address above.

### Refunds and Billing

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. For refunds, purchase history, or billing questions, use Apple's purchase support page:

https://support.apple.com/billing

## Privacy

The Human Collective does not require an account, does not use third-party advertising, and does not track you across apps or websites. If you submit a creation, the information you provide is handled as described in the privacy policy.
